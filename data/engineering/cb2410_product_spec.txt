1. Product Overview

The CB-2410 is a thermal circuit breaker designed for DC applications in industrial control panels and automotive auxiliary circuits. It provides reliable overcurrent protection with manual reset capability.

2. Technical Specifications

Rated Voltage: 24V DC
Rated Current: 10A
Breaking Capacity: 1000A at 24V DC
Insulation Resistance: >100 MΩ at 500V DC
Dielectric Strength: 1500V AC for 1 minute
Contact Resistance: <50 mΩ initial
Operating Temperature: -40°C to +85°C
Storage Temperature: -55°C to +100°C
Protection Rating: IP40 (panel mount)
Weight: 12.5g
Mounting: Snap-in panel mount, 15.8mm cutout

3. Thermal Characteristics

Trip Time at 135% In: 30-120 seconds (ambient 23°C)
Trip Time at 200% In: 4-15 seconds (ambient 23°C)
Trip Time at 600% In: 0.1-1.0 seconds
Derating Factor: -3.5% per °C above 23°C

4. Materials and Construction

Housing: Polyamide PA66, UL94 V-0 rated
Contacts: Silver-Nickel (AgNi)
Bimetal Element: High-precision bimetal strip, calibrated at factory
Terminal Type: Quick-connect 6.3mm x 0.8mm
Actuator: Toggle type, positive ON/OFF indication

5. Compliance and Certifications

UL 1077 (Supplementary Protectors)
IEC 60934 (Circuit Breakers for Equipment)
VDE certified
RoHS compliant
REACH compliant
